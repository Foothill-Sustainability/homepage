About the Sustainability Newsletter

The Foothill College Sustainability Newsletter aims to promotes positive changes made by the campus, outlines what needs to be done and which steps need to be taken in order to provide an outlook for the future of Foothill campus and beyond with regards to climate change through the eyes of students and faculty.
